About me

I am a Senior Software Engineer at Uber ATG, conducting research and development on the Perception/Prediction modules for autonomous driving.

I got my Ph.D. degree from Carnegie Mellon University, advised by Greg Ganger. My research area was system support for large-scale machine learning.
